Air Pollutants

  • CO = Carbon Dioxide
    (Colorless, odorless gas)
  • NO2 = Nitrogen Dioxide
    (Unstable triatomic form of Oxygen)
  • 03 = Ozone
    (Harmful to air quality, outside of ozone layer)
  • PM = Particle Matter
    (Solid & liquid particles suspended in air which are hazardous)
