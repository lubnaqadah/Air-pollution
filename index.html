METRICS & CRITERIA

  • ppb = parts per billion by volume
  • ppm = parts per million by volume
  • μg/m3 = milligrams per cubic meter
  • CO = Carbon Monoxide
    min level: <0 ppm | max level: >9 ppm
  • NO2 = Nitrogen Dioxide
    min level: <30 ppb | max level: >60 ppb
  • O3 = Ozone
    min level: <0.05 ppm | max level >0.2ppm
  • PM2.5 = Particulate Matter
    min level: <12 μg/m3 | max level >55 μg/m3
  • PM10 = Particulate Matter
    min level: <150 μg/m3 per 24hr | max level >300 μg/m3 per 24hr
  • SO2 = Sulphur Dioxide
    24 hour average 0.14ppm
